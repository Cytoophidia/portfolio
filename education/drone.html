Introduction

During my internship, I taught a semester-long series of lessons on drone theory and operation at Shanghai Oriental Primary School as a team leader. The course consisted of 14 sessions, all designed by our team of four undergruduate students. The course outline is as followsï¼š

  • Model making process 3D printing technology, control technology basic sensor explanation.
  • Review safety precautions, remote control, takeoff and landing methods, and practical exercises.
  • Introductory flight, fixed-point and fixed-height flight practice

  • Advanced flight function.
  • Explanation of GPS, optical flow, obstacle avoidance and positioning principles.

  • DJI aerial camera P, A, S gear operation mode and experience test flight
