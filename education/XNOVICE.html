XNOVICE

In the 'Introduction to Coding for Learning' class, we help students learn trivia about different fields, including subjects they don't like, by developing a fun game. Completing these simple but life-relevant trivia questions on various subjects, students will build a broader general knowledge. It can enhance their interest in subjects that they are not good at through game-breaking to help students get out of their subject comfort zone.

This project uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript languages including question storage, calling, and unlocking maps.
